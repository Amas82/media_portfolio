Media portfolio is a great tool to to present your portfolio at a prospective career opportunity. It is a collection of records and organize the archives and artifacts that you have created and present to your interviewer that reflect your accomplishments, most relevant skills, experiences, and attributes. It highlights and showcases samples of some of your best work, along with life experiences, values and achievements. It presents evidence of your relevant skills and abilities. It demonstrates your most compelling works as a designer but also communicates your personality to your target audience. It should grab a viewer’s interest, impress, surprise them. It can tell a client or a prospective employer about how you generate design ideas and organize the whole creative process. It is an opportunity to create a display and to launch a job search. It will help you in getting evaluated positively for the career path you have chosen. Therefore it should capture emotions and making them remember you.

I usually design my websites to express my concern about the environmental issue as a signinficant topic in today's world. I am interested in expressing my concerns about the planet awareness and designing media compaigns that show eco-friendly benefits of the products. Eco-friendly products are trending in the market as more and more people move towards a greater social awareness and healthier lifestyles. Eco-friendly products such as lipsticks, lip gloss, and lip balm that that can be in our daily basis can be harmful to the environment and even to us. From clothes, to accessories, to lotions and skincare products, to transportation to energy consumption to shopping habits, each of the items we use and our actions affect our carbon footprint and can impact the environment that we live in.

The website that I have created for 'Divine Lip Bliss' was especially meaningful to me, because it not only allowed me to show my creativity in highlighing the positive effect of lipstick, lip balm, and lip gloss. but it also allowed me to highlight nature and natural products that was used in developing these products.
